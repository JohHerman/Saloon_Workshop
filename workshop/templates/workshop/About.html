our story

a sanctuary for women, by women

born around a table,
raised by women.

éclat began in 2018 in a small living room — a few friends, coffee, and the desire to reclaim time for ourselves. What started as sunday ritual gatherings became a warm workshop space where stylists, artists and soulful women gather to learn, unlearn and connect.

Today, our atelier is a hidden courtyard full of light. We believe that beauty is a ritual, not a rush. From our hands to your hair, skin, and soul — everything is made with intention.

Every product we use is carefully selected, every technique we teach is passed down with love. We're not just a salon; we're a community of women supporting women.

"the most beautiful thing you can wear is your own energy — we just help you polish it."

— elara, founder

our journey

2018
éclat begins as a small gathering of friends in a cozy living room, sharing beauty rituals and coffee.
2020
First workshop space opens in the heart of Paris. Word spreads, community grows.
2022
Expansion to a sunlit atelier with a garden. Launch of signature rituals and organic product line.
2024
éclat becomes a full wellness sanctuary, serving hundreds of women with love and intention.
